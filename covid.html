A NOTE FROM OUR FOUNDER


We have had to postpone many of our 2020 tours due to the Covid-19 crisis. Please use this hub as a resource center as we navigate this unprecedented and rapidly changing situation together.


We want to make every effort to run your tour safely. Your dollars on any tour have a trickle-down effect, and many of our vendors in developing nations rely on this income to survive.


But our responsibility to travel safely is not just limited to keeping us safe -- we also must consider our impact on traveling in developing nations, and the risk we may inadvertently be passing to members of the vulnerable communities we visit.


As such, we will always put lives above profit. We ask for your flexibility and understanding in this endeavor.

WHAT WE'RE DOING TO KEEP YOU SAFE


We want you to book our trips with confidence, knowing that we are doing all we can amidst the global Covid-19 pandemic to help keep you healthy while traveling with us.


As a small group tour company, we’re ahead of the curve here in that some of the measures we’ve always implemented (capping tour guests, for example) will also help to keep you safer.


But we're taking it even further.


Below are some things we will be implementing in our upcoming tours.

UPDATED TOUR DETAILS

10 Person Max

Our groups have always been small (we cap our tours at 10), to help foster deeper friendships and communications. While traveling with a group may still come with risks, know that your exposure will be limited to at least our group and our drivers.

Daily Vehicle Disinfection

We’ve increased disinfection of our vehicles and transportation and are making sure that each day the car is cleaned and sanitized thoroughly after use.

Our Boutique Hotel Partners

We prefer to work with smaller and more boutique properties and will continue to do what we can to help reduce your exposure. Most of our tours include the option to upgrade to a single room, which will remain an option for you. If you are sharing a room, we will do our best to keep your roommate the same for the duration of your trip. Please note that our hotel partners are committed to intensive cleaning schedules, and as such, certain amenities of a property, such as the gym or pool, may be closed intermittently.

Our Food Partners

We like to feed you good, healthy food. Now, we’re looking at our food partners for additional support and will only be stopping at places where good hygiene is maintained. We can’t promise that every restaurant will abide by physical distancing, but we will do what we can.

Our Policy on Face Masks

We are not currently requiring travelers to wear masks on our trip, but we will ask you to bring one with you in case this changes, as well as for crowded markets, city tours and the plane to/from your destination.

Covid-19 Waivers

Prior to traveling with us, we will be sending out a waiver we will need you to sign, simply declaring that to the best of your knowledge you don’t have Covid-19 and are feeling physically healthy to trave.

Heightened Flexibility

If you need to cancel last minute because you are sick, we will apply any payments you have made towards future Damesly tours. We regret that as a small operator we cannot ensure a full refund.

Travel Insurance

Our groups have always been small (we cap our tours at 10), to help foster deeper friendships and communications. While traveling with a group may still come with risks, know that your exposure will be limited to at least our group and our drivers.

10 Person Max

This has always been a requirement on our tours, but is especially important now. Please note that we will be asking for proof of insurance prior to the start of your tour.

If You Should Become Sick

If you develop a fever on our tour, you may be asked to quarantine on trip and you may be required to have a doctor clear you before rejoining us.
