FAQ

What is Damesly?

Damesly is a boutique tour operator that combines professional + creative workshops for women, with travel to some of the world’s most amazing destinations.

Why is it just for women?

We’re a tour operator created by women, and we really believe the key to success is community. We are building a platform that allows women to help each other out, personally and professionally. Keeping our tours to women-only allows for a more comfortable environment, in which all voices, thoughts and perspectives are on an equal playing field. No question is silly here, no issue is too small or too big. We are stronger together than we are on our own and we are here to build one another up.

What will I take away from a trip?

Expert knowledge of a new industry, skill or hobby, complete with one-on-one instruction from a woman who is killing it in that field. You can also expect to gain a new community of friends to call upon for professional help, and a new pool of resources you can tap into when necessary. And of course, some seriously cool travel stories.

What’s the cancellation policy?

Many trips will offer the option of putting down a 50% deposit to secure your space. The remaining 50% must be paid 60 days prior to trip departure, once tour is confirmed.

What ages of women come on your tours?

We welcome women over the age of 21, but our typical groups tend to be made up of women in their 30s-60s. Please note we can’t predict the ages of participants on any given tour. If you’re stressed about this, don’t be! Age is just a number. All participants in good health with reasonable level of fitness are welcomed.

Do I need travel insurance?

Yes, we require all participants to have travel insurance. Proof of insurance will be requested before the tour begins

Is there a single supplement?

We hate the term “single supplement,” because most of our travelers are traveling solo! However, all tours are priced and sold based on shared accommodation unless otherwise stated. If you would like your own room, we are happy to upgrade you. Price ranges depending on destination– please reach out and let us know which tour you’re interested in if you’d like to upgrade: kelly@damesly.com

Is the workshop required?

Some tours may have include creative workshops. We structure these experiences to enhance personal + professional growth and the workshop is a key component to our trips. If you’re really not interested in the learning aspect of our tours, you do not have to attend the workshop. Though, the price is fixed as is, so take the opportunity to learn or perfect a new skill!
