WE SUPPORT WOMEN AROUND THE WORLD THROUGH TRIPS + TOURS


Damesly is a woman-owned boutique tour company offering women-only adventures, workshops, + self-discovery trips. Each tour is packed with full + exciting itineraries, designed for creative and professional women to experience the world while making new friendships.


We happily support women around the world in each travel destination through our tours.


HOW DO WE SUPPORT WOMEN?

Whenever possible we stay in women-owned hotels, eat in women-owned restaurants, work with women-owned suppliers and invest in women-owned businesses. We believe women can and do change the world.


WHY DAMESLY ?


We believe that travel is the best way to personal growth, but how does travel factor into our professional lives?


We created Damesly to bridge both worlds. Our adventures are designed to allow women to learn a new skill, perfect a new passion or just combine travel with immersion in a new community.


Our hands-on workshops feature women who lead the way in their fields. It’s all of the information you’d get in a stuffy conference, limited to a group of 20 or less, with the world as the backdrop.


We’re forming life-long personal and professional relationships, in really cool places.
